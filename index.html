What Is the ServiceNow Platform

ServiceNow is a cloud-based platform with workflow automation capabilities. The platform offers a suite of products and services that can help organizations streamline their operations, automate routine tasks, and improve general operational efficiency.

ServiceNow Benefits and Key Features

Businesses looking to achieve full self-service capabilities for the different business processes highlighted above will find ServiceNow a valuable platform for them. The platform also helps to improve the overall efficiency of service operations.

Overview of ServiceNow Products

ServiceNow is a technical management platform. It provides a range of IT solutions that allow users to manage projects, coordinate teams and customer interactions, manage workflow and deliver customer service using a comprehensive variety of apps and plugins. Some of the core products that ServiceNow offers include: ITSM, Now Platform, HR Service Delivery, IT Asset Management, Customer Service, Security Operations.
